Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our travel services

Booking & Reservations

How do I make a booking?
You can make a booking through our website by selecting your desired service (flights, buses, trains, or packages), filling in the required details, and completing the payment process. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the booking details.
Can I modify my booking after confirmation?
Yes, most bookings can be modified depending on the service provider's policy. Flight bookings typically allow changes with applicable fees, while train and bus bookings have specific modification rules. Please check your booking details or contact our support team for assistance.
What is the cancellation policy?
Cancellation policies vary by service provider and booking type. Generally, flights have different cancellation charges based on the fare type, while trains and buses have specific refund rules. Package bookings usually have a tiered cancellation policy with charges increasing closer to the travel date.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets like Paytm, Google Pay, and PhonePe. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.
Are there any hidden charges?
No, we believe in transparent pricing. The price you see during booking is the final price you pay. All applicable taxes and fees are included in the displayed price. There are no hidden charges.
How do I get a refund?
Refunds are processed according to the service provider's policy. Once a cancellation is confirmed, refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. You'll receive email notifications about the refund status.

Travel Services

Do you offer travel insurance?
Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options for domestic and international travel. Insurance covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost baggage, and other travel-related contingencies.
Can you help with visa applications?
While we don't directly process visas, we provide guidance and assistance for visa applications. We can help with documentation requirements and connect you with authorized visa service providers.
What if I need assistance during my trip?
We provide 24/7 customer support during your trip. You can contact our helpline for any assistance with bookings, emergencies, or travel-related queries. Our support team is always ready to help you.

Packages & Tours

What is included in tour packages?
Our tour packages typically include accommodation, daily breakfast, sightseeing tours, transportation between cities, and guide services. Specific inclusions vary by package, so please check the detailed itinerary before booking.
Can I customize tour packages?
Yes, we offer customizable packages. You can modify the itinerary, accommodation type, meal plans, and add or remove activities based on your preferences. Contact our travel experts for personalized packages.
