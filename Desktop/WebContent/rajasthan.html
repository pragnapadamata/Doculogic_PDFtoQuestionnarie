Discover Royal Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the land of kings, is a vibrant state in India known for its rich history, magnificent palaces, majestic forts, and colorful culture. From the golden sands of Thar desert to the Aravalli hills, Rajasthan offers a perfect blend of heritage, adventure, and royal luxury.

Popular Cities

Jaipur - The Pink City

Capital city known for its pink-colored buildings and magnificent palaces.

  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace
  • Amber Fort
  • Jantar Mantar

Udaipur - City of Lakes

Romantic city surrounded by lakes and known for its stunning palaces.

  • City Palace
  • Lake Pichola
  • Jag Mandir
  • Saheliyon Ki Bari

Jodhpur - The Blue City

Gateway to the Thar desert, famous for its blue-painted houses.

  • Mehrangarh Fort
  • Umaid Bhawan Palace
  • Jaswant Thada
  • Ghanta Ghar

Jaisalmer - Golden City

Desert city with golden sandstone architecture and camel safaris.

  • Jaisalmer Fort
  • Sam Sand Dunes
  • Patwon Ki Haveli
  • Gadisar Lake
